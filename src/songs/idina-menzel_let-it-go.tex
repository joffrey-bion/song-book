The snow glows white on the mountain tonight
Not a footprint to be seen

The cold never bothered me anyway!
\end{Chorus}

I'm free!
\end{PreChorus}

\begin{Chorus}
Let it go, let it go
I am one with the wind and sky
Let it go, let it go
You'll never see me cry!
\espaceInterStrophe

Here I stand
And here I'll stay
Let the storm rage on!
\end{Chorus}

\begin{Bridge}

I'm never going back,
The past is in the past!
\end{Bridge}
\espaceInterStrophe

\begin{Chorus}
Let it go, let it go
And I'll rise like the break of dawn
Let it go, let it go
That perfect girl is gone!
\espaceInterStrophe

Here I stand
In the light of day
Let the storm rage on,
The cold never bothered me anyway!
\end{Chorus}
